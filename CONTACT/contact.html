Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

The timeline depends on the project scope and complexity. A simple website might take 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 2-3 months. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What is your pricing structure?

I offer both fixed-price projects for well-defined scopes and hourly rates for ongoing work or projects with evolving requirements. I'll provide a transparent quote after understanding your project needs.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, I offer various support packages for maintenance, updates, and technical assistance after your project is completed. We can discuss the best option for your needs.
